Archives Resource Links

The National Archives (UK) – About Archives

This site provides detailed information about government archives, including how they preserve records and make official documents accessible for historical, legal, and administrative use.

Link: https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk

International Council on Archives (ICA) – Types of Archives

The ICA offers information on various types of archives, their roles, and the importance of preserving different kinds of records across sectors.

Link: https://www.ica.org

Millar, L. (2017). Archives: Principles and Practices

This book, published by Facet Publishing, provides foundational principles and practices in archival science. The full text is not available online, but you can purchase it from various retailers.

Purchase Links: Amazon | Facet Publishing

Gilliland, A. J. (2014). Conceptualizing 21st-Century Archives

This book explores the evolving role of archives, particularly in the digital age. While the full text may not be freely accessible online, it can be found on academic book sites or through university libraries.

Find More Information: Google Books | WorldCat (Library Finder)
